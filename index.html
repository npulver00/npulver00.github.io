Stella & Toby's World

Personal Project, January 2019

Domain: petsrus.space
GitHub: github code

Tools Used: REACT || NODE || JAVASCRIPT || REDUX || EXPRESS || POSTGRESQL || HTML5 || CCS3 || STRIPE || AUTH0 || DIGITAL OCEAN || CSS IN JS

Stella & Toby's World incorporates a small business e-commerce site that sells cat and dog products. The specific products that are being sold are food and toys. The users are able to add, delete, edit their cart that contain's their products they chose. They are also able to checkout with stripe with their final total with tax and free shipping! They are also able to add multiple addresses to their account which is connected to their Auth0 id and they can make a primary address which will render on their cart as their shipping address.


I utilized React to create the front-end framework of Stella & Toby's World. Using both React and JavaScript, I was able to create functionality throughout the app, such as being able to create, update, and delete from the cart as well as their addresses. I implemented Node.js and Express on the back-end for handling all of the RESTful API requests and connecting to the cloud-based PostgreSQL databases so that necessary user and product information could be stored. Finally, through the use of CSS and media queries, the site has a small business , friendly and warm appearance, while also being responsive to mobile devices.
